# Implementing pipeline actions

The following guides for common pipeline orchestrators are available to help you get started with implementing your continuous integration/continuous delivery (CI/CD) pipeline.

**Note:** These guides cover commonly-selected combinations of tools for the different CI/CD pipeline components, but they are intended as examples and are therefore not all-inclusive. If you do not see the specific technology or combination of tools your enterprise has selected, that does not necessarily mean it cannot be part of your IBM Z DevOps solution. For most tools being used on the distributed side, you can integrate it into the pipeline as long as it is compatible with z/OS application development and brings value to your enterprise.
